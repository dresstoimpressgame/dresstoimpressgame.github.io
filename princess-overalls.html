About Princess Overalls

Welcome to Princess Overalls, where fashion meets fantasy! Dive into a magical styling experience and become the ultimate fashion designer for beloved characters from different worlds. Your mission is to help Anna and Elsa from Arendelle, the mischievous Harley Quinn, and the adventurous Princess Moana create extraordinary outfits that showcase their unique personalities.

How to Play Princess Overalls

In Princess Overalls, you'll explore an extensive wardrobe filled with trendy clothing items. Start by selecting from our impressive collection of monochrome print jumpsuits, stylish long pants, and comfortable shorts. Mix and match these base pieces with adorable tops and vibrant sneakers to create stunning combinations.

Fashion Items Galore

Tops Collection: Choose from lovely striped t-shirts, floral print blouses, and playful graphic tees

Bottoms Selection: Experiment with denim overalls, chic shorts, and fashionable long pants

Footwear: Complete your looks with colorful sneakers and stylish shoes

Accessories: Add the perfect finishing touches with girly jewelry and matching fabric masks

Character Style Guide

Anna & Elsa: Create magical frozen-inspired outfits or modern royal looks

Harley Quinn: Design edgy, bold ensembles that match her rebellious spirit

Princess Moana: Craft ocean-themed outfits that reflect her adventurous nature

Features

  • Unlimited mix-and-match possibilities
  • Special themed backgrounds for each character
  • Outfit rating system from your clients
  • Save and share your best creations
  • Regular updates with new fashion items

Princess Overalls: Fashion Adventure for Iconic Characters

Get ready to unleash your inner stylist in Princess Overalls! Whether you're creating a royal ensemble for Elsa, a quirky outfit for Anna, a rebellious look for Harley Quinn, or an adventurous gear for Moana, your fashion skills will be put to the test. Create the most unforgettable outfits and become the most sought-after fashion designer in the princess world!
